ClipScrape

I watch livestreams a lot on twitch or youtube as second monitor stuff, and most streamers have a chat enabled. Usually, its full of spam, so I don't look at it and its just a blur of words, and I notice only the frequencies, which tends to go up when something interesting happens. This program uses this to find some of the interesting moments from a livestream
